About Match A Matcha

Match A Matcha is a personalized recipe curator for matcha lovers. Instead of scrolling endless recipe lists, you answer three quick questions and discover a drink that suits your taste as well as a pastry pairing to go with it.

Whether you're a student searching for convenience, a creative looking for cafe-style drinks at home, or a busy professional who wants something simple but special, we've got you.

What you'll find here

  • Matcha quiz to find your ideal beverage
  • Curated drink recipes with clear steps
  • Suggested pastry pairings for each drink
  • Seasonal and classic drink collections
